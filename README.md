# Financial-Performance-Dashboard
Comprehensive Tableau dashboard for financial performance analysis. Features dynamic visualizations of sales, profit, COGS, and discounts by segment, country, and product. Leverages interactive filtering and a structured dataset to deliver actionable business insights.

View on Tableau Public: https://public.tableau.com/views/FinancialPerformance_17636555402930/FinancialPerformanceDashboard?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link


Key Insights:-

-Highest sales and profit are seen in government and enterprise segments, especially in Germany and France.

-Larger discounts drive sales but can reduce profit margins.

-Peak sales and profits occur in specific months, showing seasonality.

-Profitability depends on a careful balance between sales, COGS, and discounts.
